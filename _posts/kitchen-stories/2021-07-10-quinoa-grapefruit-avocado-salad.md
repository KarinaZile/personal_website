### Ingredients:

* Quinoa
* Grapefruit
* Avocado
* Olive oil

### Methods:

* Boil quinoa
* Remove segments from grapefruit and break them up into bite size pieces.
* Cut avocados into bite size pieces.
* Mix everything and dress with olive oil.
