### Ingredients:

* Chickpeas
* Tomatoes
* Cucumbers
* Feta
* Olive oil
* Za'atar

### Methods:

* Cut tomatoes, cucumbers and feta into bite size pieces.
* Mix everything, dress with olive oil and season with za'atar.
